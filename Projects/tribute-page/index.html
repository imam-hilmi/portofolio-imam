Mesut Ozil

El Buho (The Owl Man)

This is the career journey of Mesut Ozil

  • 2005 - Played for the Schalke04 youth team at 17 years old.
  • 2006 - Played for the Schalke04 senior team as a creative playmaker and attacking midfielder. He made 30 appereance in all competitions
  • 2008 - Left Schalke04 and moved to Wender Bremen. While playing for the team, he has made 71 appearances with 30 goals and 14 assists, and won the DFB-Pokal title in the 2008 - 2009 season.
  • 2010 - Played for Real Madrid. During three seasons playing for Los Blancos, he managed to make 105 appearances with 19 goals and 80 assists, and managed to present prestigious trophies such as the Copa del Rey, La Liga, and the Spanish Super Cup.
  • 2013 - Left Real Madrid and played for Arsenal. While playing for the team, he has made 254 appearances with 44 goals and 71 assists, and became Arsenal's Player of the Year in the 2015 - 2016 season.
  • 2021 - Left Arsenal and played for Fenerbahce. He doesn't play much because he's injured a lot.
  • 2022 - Played for Istanbul Basaksehir. He is still active as a player until now.
